World Geography GeoInquiry Collection

Silks roads: Then and now GeoInquiry  

The World Geography collection supports students studying geography concepts as a part of a world studies course. Activities are aligned to the US C3 Framework for social studies.

There are 15 pre-built maps [M] supporting math classrooms - each with a short teacher guide [T] and student worksheet [S] to help you teach.

  • Number of maps: 15
  • Number of activities: 20 (including 5 level 2)
  • Target grade level:9-12
