Earth Science GeoInquiries

The Earth Science collection supports students studying Earth science concepts as a part of a natural science or physical geography course. Activities are aligned to the Next Generation Science Standards.

There are 15 pre-built maps [M] supporting Earth science classrooms - each with a short teacher guide [T] and student worksheet [S] to help you teach. 

  • Number of maps: 15
  • Number of activities: 20 (includes level 2)
  • Target grade level: 7-10
